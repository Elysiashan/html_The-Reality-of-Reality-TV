The Amazing Race

About:A group of contestants are paired in groups of two, typically the pairs are family members or loved ones. These pairs will race around the world completing competitions to advance to the next race or “leg”. After each leg the last pair to make it to the last pit stop is eliminated from the game and goes home. In each leg there are typically a few challenges either physical or mental and places they must make it to, sometimes they must ask strangers to translate and show them directions. The last team standings win a cash grand prize.

Show host: Phil Koeghan | Best seasons:12, 10, 20

There are currently 38 seasons of this show, and you can watch it on either Hulu or CBS. In addition to the show casting contestants from different backgrounds and way of life. This show also has seasons specifically for celebrites or other reality shows. For example the next season will have contestants from Big Brother and their loved ones.

Dating Shows

Love Island USA

About:Love Islandis a dating show where a group of single “islanders” are sent to the “villa” to get to know each other and form connections. These islanders are excluded from the outside world and can only interact with others in the Villa. After a few days the villa has a recoupling where they must pair up with each other and the recoupling is where they must couple up with the same connection or someone else depending on the connections. They have a few fun challenges every few episodes that cause more drama and messiness between the contestants.

Show host: Ariana Madix | Best seasons: 2, 5, 6

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Love Island USA (@loveislandusa)

Love Island has 7 seasons and are typically aired during the summer. In addition to Love Island USA, there is also the same show but in different locations like Love Island UK and Australia. The episodes are a hour long and are played every day during the summer. You can typically watch ove Island USA on Peacock.

Bachelor/Bachelorette

About:A single male or female dates many people of the opposite sex throughout the season to see who they’re most compatible with by going on one-on-one dates and having deep conversations. Each week one contestant is eliminated and by the end of the season they offer a rose to the last contestant.

Show host: Jesse Palmer | Best seasons for Bachelor: 17, 13, 28 | Best seasons for Bachelorette: 1, 11, 5

There are currently 29 seasons of the Bachelor and 21 seaons of the Bachelorette. You can watch both on Hulu and Amazon Video. In addition to the Bachelor and Bachelorette, they also have a spin off show called The Golden Bachelor where a man can find love in his golden years.

90 Day Fiancé

 About:This dating show follows American citizens and their foreign fiancés through the K-1 visa journey. The show starts with them traveling to America and starting the 90 days for them to get married until the visa expires and their journey of living together. Each couple faces many different roadblocks, being unable to work, travel, family drama, or feeling lonely in a new country.

Show host: Shaun Robinson/Sukanya Krishnan | Best seasons: 1, 2, 4

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by 90 Day Fiance (@90dayfiance)

90 Day Fiancé has 11 seasons, and the seasons typically end with the final episode or an extra episode as the tell all episode where the audience is reunited with the couples and they get asked burning questions and the stance of where their relationship is. In addition to this show, they also have many spin off series like 90 Day Fiancé: Happily Ever After which documents couples after their 90 days, and we get to see their lives happily married. You can watch many of these shows on Hulu and Disney+.

About:The Real Housewives show takes you through the personal and professional lives of affluent wives in various cities in the world. It shows the daily conflicts and realities of being a wealthy woman in the 21st century. The viewers get the chance to feel like it’s their life almost by watching these women go through difficult times and raw moments in their lives.

Show host: Andy Cohen | Best seasons: RHONY 3, SLC 2, 4

Flip or Flop

About: Business partners who were once married, work together to renovate distressed houses into nice and modern homes for families. This show documents the whole process from buying the house to figuring out how to upgrade the home. They even show potential buyers of these homes, and their goal is to sell them for higher value for profitable projects.

Show hosts: Tarek El Moussa and Christina Haack | Best seasons: 2, 1, 3

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Heather Rae El Moussa (@theheatherraeelmoussa)

Flip or Flop has 10 seasons and shows authentic and real personal struggles while also giving entertainment to the viewers. We get to see Tarek and Christina’s personal financial struggles while also seeing how they can work together and compromise. They are no longer together and they’re still business partners. The series did end but they have a new spinoff series called The Flip Off with the same hosts as before.
